About Us

A Legacy Built
on Trust

Established in 1996 in Coimbatore, Lachmi Narain Electricals is a reliable, relationship-driven distributor of electrical cables for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Located on Sungam Bypass Road - a key logistics and industrial corridor in Coimbatore.

Authorised distributor of Polycab - one of India's most trusted and widely used cable brands.

Well-stocked warehouse with fast order fulfilment and delivery support.

Part of a family business with roots going back to 1957 - our main house store in Ooty has been serving the Nilgiris region for over six decades, bringing the same trusted service to Coimbatore since 1996.
